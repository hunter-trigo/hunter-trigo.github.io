About Me

Hello! My name is Hunter Trigo. I recently graduated from the University of California - Irvine with a B.S in Computer Game Science. I am currently looking to break into the game development industry. My fields of interest lie within game design and programming. I have experience developing with Unity and creating 3D models within Maya. I have conducted playtesting for a project titled Moist Party where I was also the Art Lead. I am a hardworking and motivated individual looking to apply my knowledge and to futher develop my skills.

Interests and Skills

ArtFestival at UCI

For 4 years I have been an active member of the Art Festival at UCI. Art Festival is a club that aims to uplift the voices of womxn and LGBTQ+ members through the use of art. We coordinate safe spaces for members to join together and express themselves via various art mediums. I have been a board member since my second year and have helped coordinate and execute multiple art shows and events.

3D Modeling

I am familiar with Maya and using it to create 3D assests. I have experience creating character models,rigging them and exporting them to Unity. Examples of models I have created can be found under the Projects tab.

Unity
